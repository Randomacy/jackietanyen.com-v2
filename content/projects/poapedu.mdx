# POAPedu

A web3 edtech startup built on EDU Chain with Harish Kotra and Hazel Rodrigues — all three of us ex-colleagues from Tribe.

## the problem

Credentials are fragmented. A person's learning journey — courses, certifications, badges, bootcamps — lives across dozens of platforms with no coherent picture of what they actually know. For employers and learners alike, there's no single source of truth.

## what we built

POAPedu gave learners a universal skill profile on-chain. The core product:

- **On-chain credential aggregation** — learning certifications and badges minted as NFTs on EDU Chain, creating a verifiable, portable record of achievement
- **GenAI skill tree** — an AI layer that inferred skills from course curricula and mapped them into a structured skill tree, turning raw credentials into a career planning tool
- **Career planning layer** — learners could see skill gaps relative to target roles, and employers could assess candidates against a verifiable, structured profile
